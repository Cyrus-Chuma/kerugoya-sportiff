KERUGOYA SPORTIFF FC PROFILE

instagram,twitter,facebook @kerugoya_sportifffc

Kerugoya sportiff is a football club whose main objective is to develop football talents from a young age.For this reason the age factor of its players is as follows;U14,U17 and the senior team.It plays in the fkf central region league.It is located at kerugoya town.Its headed by Coach ZACHARY MBITI(0723244447) who is a former NSL and AFCLeopards football player.He is an accountant who is a finalist in CPA .He is currently working in INOI FARMERS SOCIETY in Kerugoya Town where he works during the day and attends training at evening.Below is his photo.There is a support system for away regional games that is conducted during home games whereby the entry fee for an adult is KSH 50 and a child KSH 20.For people willing to offer more support there will be available VIP cards costing KSH 500,KSH 1000,KSH 2000,KSH 5000 and KSH 10,000.Your support will always be greatly appreciated.
